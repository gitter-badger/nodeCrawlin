Master of Library and Information Science

The Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) at St. Catherine University — the only degree program in Minnesota accredited by the American Library Association — prepares library and information specialists to lead and influence in our rapidly changing information society.  St. Kate's MLIS students make real-world career connections, advocate for change, and advance access to information for all.

Mission statement

The Master of Library and Information Science program at St. Catherine University educates students in the principles, practices, and ethics of library and information science, imbues them with a sense of service to diverse populations, and prepares them to be life-long learners and active leaders in a rapidly changing information society.
